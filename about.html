About Me

Hello, I'm Nitish Rana, a graduate from Swami Vivekananda Subharti University in Meerut, with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications. I am deeply passionate about creating impactful designs rooted in meticulous research and thoughtful implementation.

Every morning, I kickstart my day with a steaming cup of coffee, and by noon, I unwind with a chilled soda. These moments fuel my creativity as I tackle daily design challenges with zeal, envisioning how my designs can shape the world.

I find solace in the smooth melodies of soft jazz music and the indulgence of Maryland cookies, transporting me to a realm where creativity knows no bounds.

As a creator, I specialize in crafting User Interface Designs, Photo-manipulations, Web banners, Graphics, Logos, Branding, and more, all tailored to captivate and engage users. You could say, I'm a Minimal Enthusiast.

Equipped with skills in HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Git, along with proficiency in databases like Firebase and MongoDB, I'm currently working on several projects aimed at pushing the boundaries of design and technology.
